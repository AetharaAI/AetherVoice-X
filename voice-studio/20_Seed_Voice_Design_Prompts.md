## About the “Alan Watts” / “Robert Greene” direction

You found the vibe: **Alan Watts-ish reflective philosopher** and **power / strategy / self-mastery narrator**. I would not label presets with real people’s names in the shipped product. Make them style-safe:

* Stoic Philosophical Narrator
* Reflective Sage Narrator
* Power Strategy Narrator
* Self-Mastery Documentary Voice
* Disciplined Authority Narrator

That gives you the energy without stepping into “please sue me” territory.
